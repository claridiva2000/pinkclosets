About

Dawneshia Webb is the Owner and Founder of Pink|Closets. Growing up she has always had a passion for organizing and rearranging her room.

In 2016 Dawneshia decided to step out on faith and turn her passion into a business.

Dawneshia is married with 3 wonderful kids. She has her Associates Degree in Business Administration, and she is a Certified Lisenced Wedding Coordinator.

Pink|Closets is more than just organizing your home, it's organizing your life. The vision of our company is 1 Corithians 14:40.

"But all things should be done decently and in order" Dawneshia believes that when you walk into your home and see that it's in order, it gives you a little peace of mind from things going on in your life.

Pink|Closets was founded in February of 2018. We do more than just organize closets! We offer and array of services from a small Kitchen Pantry to your large Garage. We even help with events such as weddings and parties! Just call for a free phone consultation!

Pink|Closets believes that when you walk through the doors of your home you should feel at peace. A beautiful and orderly home helps in creating a beautiful and orderly life.
